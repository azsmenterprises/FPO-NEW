Farmer Producer Organization (FPO) is a generic name, which means a group of Farmers incorporated/ registered either under Part IXA of Companies Act or under Co-operative Societies Act to gain benefits of aggregation and increase their bargaining power as they buy and sell in bulk.

Benifits of FPO

  • Collective procurement & marketing provide bargaining power to farmers Timely Supply of Quality Inputs
  • Aggregation of Quality Produce for Institutional Buyers
  • Ease of Programme Implementation, Knowledge Dissemination and Capacity Building
  • Potential for Farm Level Processing and Infrastructure development
  • Job opportunities at rural level

Guiding Values and Principles for Sustaining Farmer Producer Organizations

FPOs are based on the values of self-help, self-responsibility, democracy, equality, equity, and solidarity. FPO members must believe in the ethical values of honesty, openness, social responsibility and caring for others
