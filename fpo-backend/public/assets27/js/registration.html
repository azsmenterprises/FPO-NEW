Seed Processing Plant & Seed Producing Agency Registration

Pre-Requisites

Make ready the following informations and documents before filling the form.

For Application Of SPP Registration

  • Aadhaar Card/Pan Card/Voter Id
  • Details Of Machines and Equipments
  • Owner's Details As Mentioned In The Seed Dealer Licence
  • Seed Dealer Licence
  • District Industrial Center Certificate
  • Firm Nibandhan/Partsership Certificate (if applicable)
  • SGST Registration Certificate
  • SPP Map
  • Machine/Equipment Bill

For Application Of SPA Registration

  • Aadhaar Card/Pan Card/Voter Id
  • Owner's Details As Mentioned In The Seed Dealer Licence
  • Seed Dealer Licence
  • District Industrial Center Certificate
  • Firm Nibandhan/Partsership Certificate (if applicable)
  • SGST Registration Certificate
  • SPA/SPP Agreement
  • Contract SPP Map
